Super Dry Lager • 5% ABV

Crisp, and super dry with subtle complexity that makes for the perfect pairing with food. While maintaining an incredibly drinkable body, this beer is a floral masterpiece.

  • Hops – Magnum grown in Germany
  • Malts – German pilsner malt, Vienna malt, American grown rices
  • Yeast – Czech lager yeast

West Coast IPA • 7% ABV

Brewed in the West Coast tradition, this IPA packs a tropical and fruit forward hop profile. With a firm, yet restrained bitterness, this beer is aggressively hopped with Citra and Mosaic.

  • Hops – Citra and Mosaic grown in the Pacific Northwest
  • Malts – Canadian 2 row and rolled oats
  • Yeast – California Ale Yeast

Look Up

Washingtonia robusta, known more commonly as the Mexican fan palm, Mexican washingtonia, or Skyduster is a palm tree native to the Baja California peninsula.

These iconic 100 foot skinny palm trees line our streets and dominate our skyline. They are tall and numerous. So ingrained into the landscape of our city, it’s easy to forget they’re everywhere among us. Unless you take a beat… and look up.

Skyduster is more than a tree. It’s also a catchall for anything we might miss out on while our heads are down and our mind is elsewhere. When you drink a Skyduster, we encourage you to avert your eyes from the ads, get out of the apps, and commit the ultimate act of rebellion and simply, look up.
